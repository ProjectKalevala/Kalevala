What is Kalevala.org ?

Kalevala.org is a Layer 1 Blockchain platform that uses the latest quantum-secure encryption and artificial intelligence technologies to offer a secure, scalable, and efficient platform for decentralized finance and smart contracts. The platform utilizes a unique virtual computer system (VCS) that combines the power of blockchain with the flexibility and processing capabilities of a distributed system to provide a truly innovative solution for the decentralized economy. With KLVL, the native token of the platform, users and developers can participate in the ecosystem and take advantage of the various features and advancements offered by Kalevala.org.
